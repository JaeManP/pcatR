# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Participants must communicate respectfully, welcome constructive review,
protect respondent confidentiality, and distinguish evidence-based properties
of the pCAT from package-derived descriptive conventions.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss technical and methodological disagreements with evidence and clarity.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and disclosure of private
  information.
- Do not upload identifiable respondent data or confidential implementation
  comments.
- Correct errors transparently and document consequential changes.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `jaemanblp2@gmail.com`. The maintainer may
remove content, close discussions, or restrict participation when necessary to
protect contributors and users.
