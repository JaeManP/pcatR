# Roadmap

## Maintained in 1.x

- Stable data schema and validation behavior
- Item-level analysis and denominator transparency
- Original and updated CFIR mappings
- Publication-quality profile, respondent, and change plots
- Reproducible analysis exports and technical documentation

## Candidate future work

- Translated instrument dictionaries with documented permission and
  validation
- Additional report formats through optional Quarto templates
- Interactive action-plan tracking with explicit data-governance
  controls
- Formal user studies of usability and interpretation

A validated total score is not on the roadmap unless supported by
published measurement research.
